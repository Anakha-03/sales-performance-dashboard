# 📈 Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview

The **Sales Performance Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to monitor and analyze organizational sales performance. The dashboard provides insights into sales revenue, profit, salesperson performance, regional contribution, geographic distribution, and team-wise sales, enabling informed business decisions through interactive visualizations.

## Project Objectives

- Monitor overall sales and profit performance.
- Evaluate salesperson productivity.
- Analyze sales distribution across different geographical locations.
- Compare regional and team-wise sales performance.
- Identify monthly sales and profit trends.
- Support business decision-making using interactive dashboards.

## Business Insights

- Total sales exceeded **$6 million**, generating approximately **$5 million** in profit.
- The **APAC** region contributed the highest sales compared to the Americas and Europe.
- Sales were distributed across multiple countries, with the **USA** contributing the largest share.
- Individual salesperson analysis highlights the top revenue contributors, supporting performance evaluation.
- Monthly trend analysis reveals fluctuations in sales and profit, helping identify seasonal patterns.
- Team-wise analysis enables comparison of sales performance across organizational teams.
